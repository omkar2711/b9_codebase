Quick Career Tips

Actionable advice you can implement today

1

Tailor Your Resume for Each Application

Customize your resume to match the job description. Use keywords from the listing to improve your chances of getting past ATS systems.

2

Research the Company Before Interviews

Learn about the company's mission, values, recent news, and culture. This shows genuine interest and helps you ask thoughtful questions.

3

Build a Strong LinkedIn Profile

93% of recruiters use LinkedIn to find candidates. Make sure your profile is complete, professional, and showcases your achievements.

4

Follow Up After Every Interview

Send a thank-you email within 24 hours of your interview. It shows professionalism and keeps you top of mind for the hiring manager.

5

Practice the STAR Method for Interviews

Structure your answers using Situation, Task, Action, and Result to give clear, compelling responses to behavioral questions.

6

Keep Learning New Skills

Continuous learning keeps you competitive. Take online courses, earn certifications, and stay updated with industry trends.
